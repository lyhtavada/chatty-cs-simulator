# Live Chat Process

The standard 8-step flow for every live chat conversation.

## Step 1: Greeting

Politely greet the customer and introduce yourself.

> "Hi [Customer's Name], this is [Your Name] from Avada Support. Thanks for reaching out — I'm happy to assist you today!"

## Step 2: Show Empathy

Acknowledge the customer's situation and show understanding.

> "I totally understand how frustrating this must be. Let's work together to get this sorted out for you."

## Step 3: Probing (Very Important)

Ask specific, open-ended questions to understand the root issue. This is one of the most important steps — good probing avoids back-and-forth and resolves issues faster.

> "Could you please share a bit more details about the issue? For example, when did it happen and on which page?"

## Step 4: Set Expectation

Inform the customer about next steps and estimated handling time.

> "I'll check this for you right away. It might take about 2–3 minutes. Thanks for your patience!"

## Step 5: Troubleshoot

Investigate, analyze, and propose the best solution. Guide the customer clearly if their cooperation is needed. Refer to [case-handling.md](case-handling.md) and [escalation.md](escalation.md) as needed.

## Step 6: Update Result & Follow Up

Inform the customer of the result, whether resolved or in progress.

> "I've just fixed the issue for you! Could you please help me double-check if everything works fine now?"

## Step 7: Achieve More

After resolving the main issue, proactively offer additional help or suggest useful features.

> "Is there anything else I can assist you with today? By the way, I noticed you haven't activated [Feature X] yet — it could really help boost your [goal]. Would you like me to show you quickly?"

## Step 8: Farewell & Ask for Review (Mandatory)

Thank the customer and ask for a review. **This step is mandatory.**

> "Thank you so much for your time today! If you're happy with the support, we would truly appreciate it if you could leave us a quick review — it really helps our team continue improving."

---

## Reminders

- Always maintain a friendly, proactive, and professional tone
- Never leave the customer waiting without an update
- Follow this flow for every case, even small ones
- Asking for a review is **mandatory** — positive reviews drive installs
